### Abstract
Steganography is the art and science of hiding secret information within innocuous cover media such that the very existence of the hidden communication is concealed. Steganalysis, its counterpart, aims to detect the presence of such hidden data. This paper provides a comprehensive overview of the principal methods in both steganography and steganalysis, spanning from classical spatial-domain and transform-domain techniques to modern machine-learning and deep-learning approaches.
